Ingredients:
 - 1 pack of 6 potato scone
 - 1 tsp of tomato puree
 - 3 tbsp passata
 - 60g of mature chedder cheese

Directions:
 1. Heat oven to 180degrees C.
 2. Put potato scones on baking tray.
 3. Spread tomato puree on the potato scones.
 4. Add passata to potato scones.
 5. Grate the chedder evenly over all pieces.
 6. Put in oven for 7-10mins.
